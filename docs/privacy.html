Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Unbind ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Unbind (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Voice Recordings: When you use Unbind, you record voice journals that are processed to generate micro-tasks.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes.
  • User Preferences: Your selected challenges, preferred journaling times, and app settings.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App (screens viewed, features used, session duration).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
  • Crash Reports: Technical information about app crashes to improve stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Process your voice recordings into actionable micro-tasks using AI.
  • Personalize Experience: Tailor insights and recommendations based on your selected challenges.
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues.
  • Communicate: Send important updates about your account or the App.

3. Third-Party AI Services

Important Disclosure: Unbind uses third-party AI services to process your voice recordings:

  • OpenAI Whisper: Transcribes your voice recordings into text.
  • OpenAI GPT-4o-mini: Analyzes transcripts to generate personalized micro-tasks and insights.

Your voice recordings are:

  • Sent securely to OpenAI's servers for processing
  • Not stored permanently by OpenAI (processed and discarded)
  • Not used by OpenAI to train their AI models

By using Unbind, you consent to this data processing. You can review OpenAI's privacy practices at https://openai.com/privacy

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure.
  • Servers are located in the United States.
  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data

5. Data Retention

  • Voice Recordings: Processed immediately and not stored permanently.
  • Transcripts and Tasks: Stored as long as your account is active.
  • Account Data: Retained until you request deletion.

You can delete your data at any time through the App settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update or correct your information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using AI processing features.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@unbindapp.com

7. Children's Privacy

Unbind is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@unbindapp.com
Support: support@unbindapp.com

By using Unbind, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
